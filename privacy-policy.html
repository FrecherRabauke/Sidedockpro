Privacy Policy for SideDock Pro

Last updated: May 2026

SideDock Pro is a fully offline macOS application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, telemetry, or analytics about you or your use of the app.

The dock configuration you create — references to your apps, folders, files, and web shortcuts — is stored locally on your Mac in the app's sandbox container at ~/Library/Containers/com.sidedockpro.app/. No data is sent to us, our servers, or any third party. The app does not request network access entitlements.

When you add an app, file, or folder, the app stores a macOS security-scoped bookmark — an Apple-provided opaque reference — that lets the app open the item even after restart, while keeping the operating system's sandbox protections intact. We never read the contents of your files.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored configuration is removed by macOS along with the sandbox container.
