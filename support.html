FAQ

  • Why do the side docks disappear when an app is in full screen?
    This is an intentional macOS Space behavior. SideDock Pro respects your full-screen workflow and stays out of the way. Simply exit full-screen mode or switch spaces to access your docks again.
  • My files show a warning triangle and won't open.
    If you move or rename a file or folder that was added to the dock, macOS revokes the original access to prevent security issues. Go to the app's Settings > Permissions to re-select the file.
